NEWS

Hoochie Coochie Men is a blues-rock and folk band from the small town of Easton, PA. With musical influences that range from The Allman Brothers Band and Robert Johnson to Taj Mahal and Deertick, we have a unique sound that captivates our audience. HCM's two lead vocalists, three piece harmonies, and sound musicianship creates a big sound that is not to be forgotten.
